My Favorite Restuarants

I had eaten in many locations on Saipan, and this list will showcase some of my favorite restuarants to eat in.


Top Favorite Places:

  1. Country House Restaurant
  2. Surf's Club
  3. J's Restaurant
  4. Shirley's Coffee Shop
  5. Spicy Thai Noodle Place


Country House Restaurant | Garapan, Saipan

Country House Restaurant mainly has western style foods fitting their entire atmosphere towards America's countryside.


Things I enjoy from this place:

  • Their dinner menu
  • The indoor design/theme
  • Their services
